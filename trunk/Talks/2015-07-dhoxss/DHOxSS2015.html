Putting TEI to work

Magdalena Turska and Lou Burnard

DHOxSS 2015 Workshop Proposal

Dec 2014

Abstract

The "Guidelines" of the Text Encoding Initiative (TEI) are widely used in the production of digital scholarly editions of primary sources. This workshop combines lectures describing relevant parts of the latest version of the TEI P5 Guidelines with practical exercises reinforcing the topics covered. It includes an introduction to TEI-XML markup, explanations of relevant aspects of the TEI Guidelines, and discussion about approaches to publishing and visualizing TEI. We don't cover every part of the TEI "Guidelines", but focus on how they help you transcribe, describe, edit, and publish primary source materials in digital form.
